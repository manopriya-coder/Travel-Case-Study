Wander Together App Case Study

Designing a modern and user-friendly shopping experience to decide a travel destination with friends

Project Overview

Wander Together helps groups of friends, families, or solo travelers easily decide on a travel destination by combining preferences, budget, and interests into a collaborative decision-making experience.

Design Process

The design process started with user research and wireframing, followed by high-fidelity mockups in Figma. Colors were kept minimal, with bold product images to highlight the shoes. The app supports filtering by size, color, and brand, ensuring users can quickly find what they want.

Final Outcome

The final design is sleek, responsive, and user-centric. The Attractive background enhances product visibility and gives a premium feel. This case study highlights the balance between functionality and style, providing an enjoyable shopping journey.
